About Us

Welcome to Youth Town! A safe space and platform to help boys and girls master real-life experiences.

Our Mission

Youth Town of Tennessee offers professional residential treatment services for co-occurring mental health disorders. We seek irreversible life-change in adolescents through adventure-based, gender-responsive, empirically informed, family-focused, and thoroughly Christ-centered services.

Hope

to teens and families through an environment embodying the unconditional love of Christ.

Courage

A boys program where he will find courage to live a drug-free, and foster a relationship with the God who created him.

Healing

A girls program where she will find healing, andbe challenged, comforted, cared for and launched into sobriety and healthy-living.

Strength

As clients begin to have a glimpse of who God has created them to be, individuals are strengthened. They are able to see past their own needs and into the needs of others.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Youth Town?

Youth Town of Tennessee offers faith-based professional residential treatment services for co-occuring (substance abuse and mental health) disorders for adolescents of ages 13-17.

How do I make a referral?

You can make a referral by calling our office at 731-988-5251 or sending an email to emathews@youthtown.net

How long is a residential stay at Youth Town?

The average stay for a resident at Youth Town is about 4 months.
